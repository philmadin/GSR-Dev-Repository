You’re placed into the shoes of a young goddess named Divine. With no immediate direction and a story that feels like it was rushed, you’re left to explore the world. <br />
<br />
Most of the story is “left open to interpretation”, with the only solid facts being a great war started by a dark king in the realm of the gods. With a healthy dose of memory loss, Divine sets off to recover her lost memories and find out what has happened to her home. <br />
<br />
The cliché riddled narrative is told poorly through mediocre voice acting and horrid text. For a game that prides itself on an emotive and symbolic experience, the unengaging storyline blemishes the game entirely.
