<br>Something that makes <i>Watch_Dogs 2</i> stand above it's predecessor is in the graphics department. With no graphic downgrade controversy overshadowing it's looks, <i>Watch_Dogs 2 </i>displays a great open world environment with fantastic attention to details and diversity.<p><br></p><p>Their are a few areas such a reflections and shadows that can be improved but overall it is a fantastic game to look at. <span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);">The more vibrant colour palette and unique districts ranging from Silicon Valley to the bay area show off the great graphical engine behind the proceedings.</span><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51);"> NPC models and main character models have also been given a boost, reacting to your selfie with a well timed photo bomb or abusive punch to the face. </span></p>
