An infamous villain called the Scarecrow finally emerges from the shadows and launches a deadly assault on the citizens of Gotham. After drowning in fear, civilians flee the city as it morphs into the perfect jungle for criminals and evil masterminds alike. <br />
<br />
Despite the Batmobile having a bumper sticker with “been there, done that”, another lethal adversary arises to challenge Batman – The Arkham Knight. As the Scarecrow works alongside this new antagonist, they lead an army of terrifying villains to plot against Batman.<br />
