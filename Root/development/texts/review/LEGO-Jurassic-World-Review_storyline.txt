Set on a tropical island known as Isla Nublar or “Cloud Island”, an attraction known as Jurassic Park has been fashioned. Populated with hazardous cloned dinosaurs means a disaster is just waiting to happen. Key characters like Dr Alan Grant, Ian Malcolm and Owen Grady lead the charge in this prehistoric adventure. <br />
<br />
Roughly following the story of the films, the game includes the most notable moments from each movie. Toned down for younger audiences, scenes have been altered to be less violent in favour of comedy; perhaps only the older fans will recognise changes made to the story.<br />
