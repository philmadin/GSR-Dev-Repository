In addition to layers of soundtrack creating the right atmospheres at the right time on a sub-atomic level of detail, procedurally-generated music in challenge rooms and realistic acoustics in each area cement Ninja as a triumph aurally. The incredible foley matches the cartoonish style of the game well, and voice-overs and cutscenes are always convincing. <br />
<br />
The sources of in-game sounds and who they are heard by are represented by radiating rings, which allow the player to tweak their play style to an incredible degree in order to use sounds such as their footsteps to their advantage.
