Popped on a set of headphones and fell in love with the surrounding environments of HITMAN. From the whistling breeze outside to the lousy misstep in the kitchen; the satisfying bullet-spray and the auctioneer's boring pitching.<br />
<br />
The elegance of the audio here is superb in many ways, but can only be fully experienced with some decent headphones - that is unless your TV's volume is turned to 100.
