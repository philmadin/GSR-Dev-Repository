As players uncover clues throughout the game, they will slowly learn that there is more to it than meets the eye. Adding to the atmosphere, is Dr Hill, a psychiatrist that breaks the fourth wall to interrogate the player on their greatest fears. Answers partially determine early scares and character actions which then increases the level of tension.<br />
<br />
At its heart, Until Dawn, pays homage to traditional horror films in an entertaining way. Intense thrills and snap decisions drive players into deciding who lives throughout the night. With numerous collectibles and details to find, it will take more than one round to thoroughly complete the game. <br />
