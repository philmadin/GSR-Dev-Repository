From redefining stealth gameplay with the original MSX title, to breaking the fourth-wall with the mind-bending Metal Gear Solid, and trolling players the world over with Raiden in Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty; creator Hideo Kojima has relished in throwing gamers in the deep end, whilst providing genuinely innovative gameplay. <br />
<br />
After recent tumultuous events that have resulted in Kojima Productions being culled from Konami, it’s a somewhat bittersweet event that we now have Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain – possibly the final entry to the legendary franchise.<br />
